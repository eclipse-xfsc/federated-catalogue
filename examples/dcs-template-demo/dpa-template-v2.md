The Processor implements the measures described in Annex II of this DPA, in accordance with Article 32 GDPR. The
Processor shall review and, where appropriate, update these measures at least annually.

## 6. Cross-border transfers

Personal data shall not be transferred outside the EU/EEA without an appropriate transfer mechanism under Chapter V
GDPR. Where Standard Contractual Clauses apply, the parties incorporate the version in force at the time of transfer.

## 7. Audit rights

The Controller may audit the Processor's compliance with this DPA upon reasonable prior notice, no more than once per
calendar year, unless a personal-data breach or supervisory-authority request justifies an additional audit.

## 8. Personal-data breach notification

The Processor shall notify the Controller without undue delay, and in any event within 48 hours, after becoming aware of
a personal-data breach, providing the information required under Article 33(3) GDPR.
